Preprocessing choices

  • Family mapping: normal, DoS, Probe, R2L, U2R.
  • One-hot encoding: protocol, service, flag.
  • MinMax scaler fitted on training data only.
  • Engineered byte, service, error-gap, and login signals.

Interpretation: R2L and U2R are rare and subtle. A model can look good globally while failing these classes, so per-family recall is necessary for an IDS defense.

No test leakage: test statistics are not used in preprocessing3 / 12
Experimental Design
Baseline + variations + improvements

From Baseline IDS To Robust Explainable Ensemble

1 Baseline

Logistic Regression gives an interpretable lower bound.

2 XAI Models

Random Forest + TreeSHAP, Torch MLP + IG.

3 Binary IDS

Binary RF, ExtraTrees, XGBoost, Torch Binary MLP.

4 Robustness

Multi-eps PGD fine-tuning and SHAP-guided adversarial augmentation.

5 Final

Adv+ExtraTrees Ensemble with robustness-aware threshold.

Why ensemble?

The neural model is differentiable and useful as a surrogate, but vulnerable to direct PGD. The tree model is less aligned with neural gradients, reducing transferability.

Final weights

0.15 adversarial Torch Binary MLP + 0.85 SHAP-Robust ExtraTrees, threshold 0.15.

Can Explanations Be Weaponized?

Short interpretation

SHAP and IG explanations are useful for analysts, but they also identify features an attacker can perturb. This is why the report tests explanation-guided evasion instead of treating XAI as only a visualization step.

Standalone models are vulnerable: RF SHAP reaches 18.22% evasion and Torch IG reaches 15.97%.

Limitations
How to avoid overclaiming

What The Results Mean, And What They Do Not Mean

Dataset limits

  • NSL-KDD is old and not modern enterprise traffic.
  • KDDTest+ includes train/test distribution shift.
  • R2L and U2R remain rare and difficult.

Oral phrase: strong result, not solved problem.

Adversarial limits

  • Full-feature PGD is a worst-case feature-space test.
  • Mutable-feature PGD is more realistic but not packet generation.
  • Robustness is empirical, not certified.
  • Heavy XAI is not real-time friendly.

Why this helps: precise limitations make the defense more credible.
